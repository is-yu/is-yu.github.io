major 2022 arXiv

Hierarchical Text-Conditional Image Generation with CLIP Latents

Aditya Ramesh, Prafulla Dhariwal, Alex Nichol, Casey Chu, Mark Chen

Summary

DALL-E 2 demonstrated that diffusion models conditioned on CLIP embeddings can generate high-quality images from text, making AI art commercially viable.

Key Findings

Finding 1
Combining CLIP text-image embeddings with diffusion models enables generation of highly realistic and diverse images from natural language descriptions, with fine-grained control over composition and style.
Proved that text-to-image generation could reach commercially viable quality, launching a revolution in AI-generated visual content.

Context

DALL-E 2 built on diffusion models (Ho et al. 2020), CLIP (Radford et al. 2021), and classifier-free guidance. It was quickly followed by Stable Diffusion and Midjourney, making AI image generation mainstream.
