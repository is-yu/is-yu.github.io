Improving Language Understanding by Generative Pre-Training

Alec Radford, Karthik Narasimhan, Tim Salimans, Ilya Sutskever

Summary

GPT demonstrated that generative pre-training on unlabeled text produces transferable language representations, launching the foundation model era.

Key Findings

Finding 1
A Transformer trained with autoregressive language modeling on a large unlabeled corpus learns representations that transfer to diverse NLP tasks with minimal fine-tuning.
Established the pre-train then fine-tune paradigm that unified NLP under a single framework.

Context

GPT showed that generative pre-training on next-token prediction creates powerful general-purpose representations, complementing BERT's bidirectional approach released months later.
