Is there a more parallelizable alternative to RNNs for sequence modeling?

Context

RNNs (including LSTMs) process sequences step-by-step, making them inherently sequential and slow to train. Could an architecture process all positions simultaneously while still capturing long-range dependencies?

Breakthroughs

Attention Is All You Need
paradigm-shift 2017 · Ashish Vaswani et al. · NeurIPS 2017
The Transformer replaced RNNs with self-attention for fully parallel sequence processing, becoming the foundation for all modern large language models.
