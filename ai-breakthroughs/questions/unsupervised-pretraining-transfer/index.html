Can unsupervised pre-training on text transfer effectively to all NLP tasks?

Context

Traditional NLP required task-specific architectures and labeled data for each problem. Could a single model pre-trained on unlabeled text learn general language understanding transferable to any downstream task?

Breakthroughs

Improving Language Understanding by Generative Pre-Training
paradigm-shift 2018 · Alec Radford et al. · OpenAI
GPT demonstrated that generative pre-training on unlabeled text produces transferable language representations, launching the foundation model era.
BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers for Language Understanding
paradigm-shift 2018 · Jacob Devlin et al. · NAACL 2019
BERT showed bidirectional pre-training produces superior language understanding, making a single model competitive across all NLP benchmarks.
