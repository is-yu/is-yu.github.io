Can very deep networks be trained without performance degradation?

Context

As networks got deeper beyond ~20 layers, training accuracy actually decreased — not from overfitting but from optimization difficulty. Simply stacking more layers did not improve performance.

Breakthroughs

Deep Residual Learning for Image Recognition
paradigm-shift 2015 · Kaiming He et al. · CVPR 2016
ResNet introduced skip connections enabling training of 100+ layer networks, solving the depth degradation problem and becoming a ubiquitous building block.
