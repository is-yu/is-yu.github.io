Can language models learn to use external tools to overcome their inherent limitations?

Context

LLMs are limited by their training data cutoff, inability to do precise math, and lack of real-time information. Could they learn to call APIs, search engines, and calculators to augment their capabilities?

Breakthroughs

Toolformer: Language Models Can Teach Themselves to Use Tools
major 2023 · Timo Schick et al. · NeurIPS 2023
Toolformer proved LLMs can self-teach tool use, enabling them to overcome inherent limitations by calling external APIs when needed.
