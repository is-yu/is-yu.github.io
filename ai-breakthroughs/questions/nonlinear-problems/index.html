Can perceptrons solve non-linearly separable problems like XOR?

Context

Minsky and Papert showed that single-layer perceptrons cannot represent XOR or other non-linearly separable functions. This raised the question of whether neural networks were fundamentally limited.

Breakthroughs

Perceptrons: An Introduction to Computational Geometry
paradigm-shift 1969 · Marvin Minsky et al. · MIT Press
Proved single-layer perceptrons cannot solve non-linear problems, contributing to the first AI winter but also motivating multi-layer research.
Learning representations by back-propagating errors
paradigm-shift 1986 · David Rumelhart et al. · Nature
Backpropagation made multi-layer neural networks trainable, solving the credit assignment problem and launching the connectionism revival.
